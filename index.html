How Tokenization Drives Output

  • Tokenization: Text is not read as words, but broken into numerical units called tokens.
  • Prediction: The model calculates the statistical probability of the next token.
Lectern-generated talk track

Think of it like this. Imagine you're teaching a robot to read, but instead of giving it a dictionary of complete words, you give it a box of letter magnets. That refrigerator is essentially what a tokenizer is — it breaks your text into pieces the model can work with mathematically. When you type “Hello, how are you?”, the model doesn't see those words at all. It sees subword tokens…

Private by design

Your slides, your API key, your Mac. Lectern has no servers, stores no content, and ships no analytics.
